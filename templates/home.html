Common questions

Can parents register themselves?

Yes. Once your school is on ESA, parents and students choose your school from the list and create their own accounts.

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?

No. Register your school on the free tier and upgrade later when you need Standard or Premium features.

Is our school data kept separate?

Yes. ESA is multi-tenant — each school’s users, classes, and records are scoped to that school only.

What about fee payments?

Standard and Premium plans support Stripe-powered online payments so parents can pay school fees securely.
